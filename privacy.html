Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

SUAVO ("we", "our", "the app") is a personalized AI dating and relationship coach that helps users improve their communication skills. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. If you use Apple Sign-In, we receive the information you authorize Apple to share.

Profile preferences: During onboarding, you provide communication preferences to personalize your coaching experience.

Contact information: You create contact profiles within the app containing names or nicknames, platforms, and notes about people you are communicating with. This data is stored in your account and is never shared. No real names are required — nicknames and initials are encouraged.

Conversation data: When you use the coaching features, your messages to SUAVO and the AI coaching responses are stored to maintain conversation continuity, build personalized coaching context, and improve your experience over time.

Outcome data: You may log outcomes (such as dates scheduled) to track your progress. This data is used to personalize coaching and display your stats.

Conversation Context and Personalization

SUAVO remembers the context of your conversations so the coaching gets more relevant over time. All conversation context is provided by you — we do not access your contacts, messages, phone, or any external data source. This context is your personal record of your coaching experience, stored for your benefit.

As you use SUAVO with a specific contact, the app builds personalized coaching context based on conversations you share. This allows SUAVO to provide increasingly specific and relevant advice. You can delete any contact and all associated coaching context at any time.

Screenshot Processing

Screenshots are processed in memory and immediately discarded. We never store, save, or retain the images you upload. Only the text extracted from screenshots is temporarily used to provide coaching, and the original images are deleted from memory immediately after processing.

When you upload screenshots for analysis, they are sent to our AI provider for text extraction only. The images are not stored on our servers, in our database, or in any persistent storage. We do not retain copies of your screenshots.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized coaching based on your preferences and conversation context
  • To maintain conversation history within the app for coaching continuity
  • To build personalized coaching context that improves over time
  • To display your progress and statistics
  • To process subscription payments through Apple
  • To improve the quality of our coaching through aggregated, anonymized usage patterns

AI Processing

SUAVO uses artificial intelligence to analyze conversations and generate coaching suggestions. Your conversation data is sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) for processing. This data is used solely to generate your coaching responses and is subject to Anthropic's data processing terms, which prohibit using customer data for model training.

The AI coaching is personalized to your communication style and the context of your conversations. The AI does not make decisions on your behalf — all suggestions are recommendations that you choose whether to use.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides encryption at rest and in transit. We use row-level security policies to ensure you can only access your own data. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is shared only with:

  • Anthropic: Our AI provider, for processing coaching requests. Subject to their data processing agreement.
  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider. Subject to their data processing agreement.
  • Apple: For subscription payment processing through the App Store.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. Receives only subscription status, not conversation data.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. Contact profiles you archive remain in the system to preserve your coaching history and statistics, but are hidden from your active view.

If you delete your account, all personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days. You can also delete individual contacts and all associated data at any time from within the app.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Delete individual contacts and their associated coaching context
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of any non-essential data processing

Children's Privacy

SUAVO is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we learn that we have collected data from a person under 18, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
