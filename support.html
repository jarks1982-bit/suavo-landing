Frequently Asked Questions
Suavo is an AI-powered dating and relationship coaching app. You share conversation context — by pasting messages or uploading screenshots — and Suavo reads the situation, builds a behavioral profile of the person you're talking to, and coaches you on what to say, when to say it, and when to say nothing. It's not a message generator. It's a coaching layer that helps you understand her so you can show up as the best version of yourself.
Yes. Screenshots you upload are processed in memory and immediately discarded — they are never stored on our servers. Conversation data you share is stored securely in your account using encrypted storage with row-level security, meaning only you can access your data. Your data is sent to Anthropic (our AI provider) solely to generate coaching responses — they do not use it for model training. We never sell or share your personal data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
The free plan gives you access to 2 contacts and 5 coaching sessions per day — enough to try the full experience. Premium ($11.99/month) gives you unlimited contacts, unlimited sessions, full Suavo Knows behavioral insights, complete outcome timeline, and access to all coaching modes including Date Mode. You can upgrade anytime from within the app.
Suavo subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel: open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Suavo → Cancel Subscription. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period. We do not offer refunds for partial subscription periods, but you'll retain Premium access until the period ends.
You can delete your account directly in the app. Go to Settings → scroll to the bottom → Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm. Once confirmed, all your data — contacts, coaching history, sessions, and account — is permanently deleted. This cannot be undone. If you have a Premium subscription, cancel it in your Apple Settings first to avoid future charges.
